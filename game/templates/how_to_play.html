HOW TO PLAY

TradeLeague is a competitive trading simulation. Analyze markets, allocate capital, and climb the leaderboard.

01

CREATE OR JOIN

  • Start a new game room from the lobby or enter a room code.
  • Share the room link with friends to play 1v1.
  • Each room supports head-to-head format.
02

WAIT FOR OPPONENTS

  • Minimum 2 players required to begin.
  • Host starts the game once opponent joins.
03

ANALYZE MARKET

  • Study real-world asset data, charts, and indicators.
  • Use built-in analysis tools to evaluate risk.
  • Compare assets before committing capital.
04

INVEST WITHIN TIMER

  • Allocate virtual funds across assets before the clock runs out.
  • Diversify or go all-in — every decision impacts your score.
  • Adjust positions as conditions shift.
05

RESULTS & RANKINGS

  • Profits and losses calculated instantly.
  • View detailed performance breakdown vs opponents.
  • Rankings update in real time on the leaderboard.
06

CLIMB THE LEAGUES

  • Earn ranking points based on profit and consistency.
  • Progress through league tiers as skill improves.
  • Compete for top positions on global leaderboard.

PRO TIPS

▸ Diversify — don't put all capital into a single asset.
▸ Watch trend indicators and volatility before investing.
▸ Review trade history after each round to find patterns.
▸ Consistency matters — steady gains rank higher than risky swings.
!

This is a SIMULATED TRADING ENVIRONMENT for educational purposes only. No real money involved.
